\section{Introduction}

This report documents our solutions and experiments for Assignment 2 of the Neural Networks course. The assignment is divided into two primary parts:
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Part I} focuses on applying Multi-layer Perceptrons (MLP), Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to the ReducedMNIST dataset, and recognizing speech segments from spectrogram images.
    \item \textbf{Part II} focuses on representing speech data using Autoencoders (AE), conducting a comprehensive Data Augmentation study on ReducedMNIST, and generating synthetic data using Generative Adversarial Networks (GAN).
\end{itemize}

Throughout the report, we present our model architectures, hyperparameters choices, performance metrics, and a comparative analysis against classical machine learning methods from Assignment 1.
