Insights and observations gained from experiments:
- The previous anomaly where Attention took less time and had lower accuracy was purely a false negative caused by EarlyStopping triggering prematurely due to minor loss fluctuations.
- Forcing both models to train for exactly 35 epochs proves that Attention genuinely adds computational time but yields a better (or matching) semantic understanding of the audio features.

Suggestions for future improvements:
- Channel Attention (SENet Squeeze-and-Excite) added alongside Spatial Attention would also learn WHICH mel frequency bands matter most per digit.
- Fine-tuning a pre-trained audio backbone (YAMNet) on this dataset would likely achieve near-perfect accuracy.
